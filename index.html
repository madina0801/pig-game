How to play ?

  • first turn for player 1
  • you can ROLL DICE until you get 1 in dice
  • once you got 1 in dice turn is moved to another player
  • your score will be store in temporary area and when you hold your score is added into main score if you got zero in dice then temporary score get zero and turn move to another player
